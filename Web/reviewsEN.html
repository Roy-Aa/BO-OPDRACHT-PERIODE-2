Reviews

Iwan Bijmholt

Been with 4 boys (10 to 12 years). We all enjoyed. Fun games and lots nostalgia (at least for me). We will definitely go again, only from Groningen it is a bit of a drive, but well worth it.

M.l. de Bruijn

I was amazed, what a super nice museum. Great to get started with this one games. It was like going to an arcade without all those guilders and then we have it not even about the retro game consoles that are plentiful. I hope we see a lot more to get.

Frank Pronk

A place where you can go back in time, from Atari to PlayStation. Is here enough too do for young and old. The arcade cabinets also ensure that in a podracer racing or Tekken play against people. Take a look here and be overwhelmed by the game world.
